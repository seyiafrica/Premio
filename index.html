The process

How we deliver the full basket

Four steps, eight hours from order to doorstep. Every Premio order flows through the same temperature-controlled, slot-scheduled system.

01
Browse & build your basket
Shop across sixteen departments on app, web, or WhatsApp. Add anything from a carton of water to a ribeye steak. Minimum order ₦5,000.
02
Choose your 30-minute slot
A seven-day calendar with live capacity. Morning slots from 6:30am. Peak-hour slots carry a small surcharge. Lock it by 11pm the night before.
03
Picked & packed after midnight
Your order is picked overnight in our Abule Egba cold warehouse. Chilled, ambient, and frozen items go into separate insulated crates.
04
Tracked to your door
Follow your van live on the map. Driver calls 15 minutes before. Arrives in your 30-minute window. Unpacks onto your counter if you ask.
